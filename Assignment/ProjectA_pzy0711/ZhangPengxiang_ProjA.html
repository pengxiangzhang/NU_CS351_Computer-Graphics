User’s Guide:
The rate of the water coming out from the pipe (Spin of the 3D object) can be controlled using the keyboard with keys “W” and “S” or “↑” and “↓”, It can also be controlled using the input on the top of the webpage.
The rate of the water flow (moving speed of the 3D object) can be controlled using the keyboard with keys “A” and “D” or “←” and “→”. You may see the speed of the water flow on the top of the webpage.
You may also drag and spin using the mouse on the webpage to rotate the 3D object.
